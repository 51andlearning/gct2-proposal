# Assumptions

The proposal — and this site — are built on the following assumptions. Every recommendation is **directional** until the assumptions below are validated against GCT's internal data post-approval.

## Data access
- **DSG & MVNE have no live access to GCT's internal data** (subscriber base, ARPU, churn, billing systems, distribution P&L).
- All figures presented (eSIM attach rates, addressable subscribers, Bitdefender margins, PWA hours) are based on:
  - DSG / MVNE benchmark data from comparable African MVNOs.
  - The pricing models supplied by DSG (`Bitdefender Commercials.xlsx`, `Travel eSIM Pricing Model.xlsx`, `Client MVNO PWA Support - Pricing Model V4.xlsx`).
  - Public information on GCT Group and Nigerian telecom market sizing.

## Strategy & directional sizing
- The 60-month business plan, GTM strategy, and revenue projections are **directional only**.
- The Travel eSIM model uses base-case attach rates of 3% (Yr 1) ramping to 8% (Yr 5) against a target market of 5.5M – 6.7M outbound travellers. These rates are illustrative — actual attach is sensitive to:
  - GCT's outbound traveller share.
  - In-app placement and CRM cadence.
  - Pricing vs. legacy IDD roaming.
- Bitdefender margin (60%) and eSIM net margin (30%) assume the commercial structure in the supplied pricing models holds; ROE = 16.9 ZAR/USD as at the date of the model.

## Validation required post-approval
After acceptance, all figures must be re-baselined using GCT's actual data during the **DBOT Design phase (8 weeks)**:
- Subscriber base, ARPU, churn, payment behaviour.
- Existing channel and reseller footprint (for MARS).
- Voucher / recharge volumes (for VMS).
- IT estate, network interconnects and BSS/OSS readiness.
- Legal / regulatory constraints (NCC, data protection).

## Commercial assumptions
- USD as the contracting currency for all commercial terms shown.
- Setup fees billed monthly across the first 6 months unless otherwise stated.
- Revenue-share products (Travel eSIM = 10% to GCT) settle monthly net of platform costs.
- All pricing excludes VAT, withholding tax and FX hedging — to be agreed in the SoW(s).

## Out of scope (assumed)
- Spectrum acquisition.
- Network CAPEX (radio, transport, core).
- Procurement of physical SIM, devices or retail estate beyond what each product specifies.
